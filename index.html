National Academic Assessment Drive  —  Safety Edition (NAAD)

Ghana’s national safety education initiative — where storytelling meets action. Through Fire Service Tales, students learn fire safety, emergency response, and civic responsibility, guided by the Ghana National Fire Service and key national institutions. Under the NAAD Safety Edition, every student is assessed, celebrated, and rewarded — with outstanding performers earning national recognition at the prestigious Cadet Personality Awards.
