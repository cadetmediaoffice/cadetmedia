🇬🇭 National Public Safety Quiz

Cadet Media Ghana  ·  2026 Edition

This competition tests your understanding of Ghana's public safety institutions and the vital roles they play in protecting lives, maintaining order, and building safer communities. You will be assessed across 12 dedicated service modules — each representing a key national agency working to keep Ghana safe.

🏆 Outstanding performers will be honoured and awarded at the Cadet Personality Awards Day held at the Fire Academy and Training Centre (FATS).

Certificates of achievement are awarded to all participating cadets.
🥇 Gold — 85% & above 🥈 Silver — 70–84% 🥉 Bronze — 50–69%

📚 Service modules in this competition:

  • 🛡️ General Public Safety & Emergency Awareness
  • 🔥 Fire Safety & Prevention — Ghana National Fire Service
  • 👮 Community Policing — Ghana Police Service
  • 🚑 Health & First Aid — National Ambulance Service
  • ⚖️ Discipline & Corrections — Ghana Prisons Service
  • 🌪️ Disaster Preparedness — NADMO
  • ✈️ Border & Travel Safety — Ghana Immigration Service
  • 📦 Trade & Customs — Ghana Revenue Authority
  • 🏛️ Heritage, Tourism & Arts — Ministry of Tourism, Arts and Culture
  • 🌿 Environmental Protection — Forestry Commission
  • 🎖️ National Defence — Ghana Armed Forces
  • 🚫 Drug & Substance Awareness — NACOC
An educational initiative by Cadet Media Ghana in collaboration with Ghana's national public safety institutions
