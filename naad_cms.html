📖 Question Writing Guidelines

Please read before submitting to ensure questions meet NAAD standards.

✅ Good Questions

✔ Factually accurate — based on official agency guidelines and national safety standards
✔ Four plausible options — all four answers should seem possible to a student
✔ One clear correct answer — no ambiguity
✔ Strong explanation — clearly explain WHY the answer is correct with facts or laws
✔ Scenario-based — e.g. "A student sees smoke — what should they do?"

❌ Avoid These

✗ Trick questions or deliberately confusing wording
✗ Questions where two or more answers could be correct
✗ Politically sensitive or culturally inappropriate content
✗ Duplicate questions already in the NAAD system
